## Authority Boundary

KeeperHub does not provide ClearIntent authority approval. It is the execution/orchestration layer after ClearIntent verification. The current midpoint evidence proves programmatic workflow submission, not final execution, onchain settlement, or policy approval by KeeperHub.

Closeout can proceed as workflow-execution proof if the hackathon claim stays limited to KeeperHub orchestration after ClearIntent verification; onchain execution claims require a separate transaction-backed run.
