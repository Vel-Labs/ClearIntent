## Project-useful facts

- ENS explicitly provides `llms.txt` and `llms-full.txt` for AI-assisted development.
- Text records are the natural place for ClearIntent policy and audit pointers.
- ENSIP-25 is draft and specifically targets AI agent registry verification through ENS text records.
- Universal Resolver and CCIP Read support matter for modern resolution and offchain/L2 records.
- Subnames are the clearest path for role-specific identities.
